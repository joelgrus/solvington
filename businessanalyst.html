Exceptional Business Analyst

Solvington is looking for a highly analytical, talented and driven business analyst for an interesting team project. The ideal candidate will be someone looking to learn and grow rapidly as a management consultant under the tutelage of the company founder, a senior leader with an 15 year exceptional track record of creating business value through leading consulting firms such as Deloitte and Point B.

This is your ideal opportunity if you are

  1. An early-career professional (1-3 years of work experience), who is highly motivated to develop themselves into an exceptionally valuable analyst, project manager and business leader
  2. A highly analytical person who looks critically at problems and enjoys the process of deconstructing issues and defining solutions
  3. A sharp, articulate professional, who is looking to exponentially grow their ability to develop analyses, tell compelling stories that drive major decisions and engage with a variety of audiences effectively
  4. Someone who has worked with enterprise systems or can conceptually understand how technology systems are used to drive business operations
  5. Someone who views education as an ongoing journey, and believes one can never stop learning. Your specific academic qualifications are far less important than your rabid curiosity and ability to quickly learn new material
  6. Someone who reads voraciously about business and technology and has well-thought out opinions
  7. Someone who has tasted success and failure and can appreciate the value and learning each experience brings. Someone who can discuss their experiences with maturity, wisdom and optimism for the future
  8. Someone who likes to create - be it a blog post, a research paper, a story telling or data visual, a website, short story, novel, textbook - you name it
  9. Someone who believes they are CEO of their life and consider freelance opportunities (1099) the wave of the future for talented professionals who desire autonomy, mobility and growth

Bottom line, if you are an early career professional who is looking to launch their career into the next orbit, this is your opportunity!

If this sounds like you, contact us at: info@solvington.com
